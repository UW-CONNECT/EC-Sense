This section has necessary resources for re-creating EC-Sense Tag. The Tag hardware design including PCB files can be found in Tag Hardware folder.

TAG COST : We use off-the shelf components in our Tag design thereby reducing the overall cost. Diodes for the EC block would cost around $12 for 12-stage board with 24 diodes. The total cost of capacitors and inductors is around \$3. The energy sensing block cost about $12. Finally, the cost of microcontroller and radio chip is around $3. 915MHz whip antennas cost about $6, however the cost depends on gain and the frequency range.
The total cost of our Tag excluding antennas comes to \$30. The cost of the Tag can be lower if ordered in bulk quantities.
